Total Asset Analysis

By analyzing Beximko’s total asset being an investor view I can know their progress. From the graph their T.A. has increased gradually, that means they are running their business firmly by using the resource accordingly

Total Liability and Owners Equity Analysis

A firm T.L. should not exceed its T.A. in this analysis T.L. breakings can be seen similarly as T.A. And it is seen that Beximko is managing its T.L and equity simultaneously.

Comprehensive Income Analysis

Comprehensive income is mainly the variation in a company net asset from non-owner sources for a specific period. This analysis is done because Beximko’s revenues are generated by both local and foreign sources. By this we can measure the amount difference.

COGS Analysis

COGS analysis is an important tool. By this gross profit is calculated. If COGS is more than sales revenue than gross profit will become lower in case loss too. By analyzing it I can find out their gross profit and other incomes.

Sales Revenue Analysis

By analyzing the revenue for 5 years their constant increase in revenue can be seen. Here I have also include their product category, so that it can be easy to find out the source of this revenue.
